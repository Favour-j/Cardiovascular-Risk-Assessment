## ğŸ§  Business Problem & Insights

### â“ Problem Statement

Cardiovascular diseases remain one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide. Healthcare providers and analysts often face challenges in **identifying high-risk patient groups**, understanding how **age and clinical factors impact survival**, and communicating these insights clearly for data-driven decision-making.

Raw patient data alone makes it difficult to:

* Detect survival patterns across age groups
* Understand how clinical indicators (ejection fraction, serum creatinine) change with age
* Assess the combined impact of lifestyle and medical conditions on mortality risk

This dashboard was built to **transform complex cardiovascular health data into actionable insights**.

---

### ğŸ’¡ Business & Analytical Insights

Using interactive visuals and KPI-driven analysis, the dashboard reveals that:

* **Survival rates decline significantly with increasing age**, with the sharpest drops observed in older age groups.
* **Average ejection fraction decreases as age increases**, indicating worsening cardiac efficiency among higher-risk patients.
* **Serum creatinine levels tend to rise in older age groups**, suggesting increased kidney strain, which correlates with lower survival outcomes.
* Patients with **underlying conditions such as hypertension, diabetes, anaemia, and smoking habits** show noticeably lower survival distributions, especially when combined with older age.
* Certain age groups contribute disproportionately to total deaths, helping stakeholders focus preventive care and early intervention strategies.
